Unsure how to validate your assumptions or move forward with your product strategy?

Try the Product Management Board (PMBoard) to use quantitative and qualitative data to answer questions like:

  • Who are our users and what are their biggest problems?
  • What should we build to support them and which features are the most important?
  • What's the best way to reach our intended users and how could we make money?
  • How are we performing according to our primary metrics and what can we do about it?
